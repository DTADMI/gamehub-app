SS1 Oasis Caravan Circle (Sahara & Sahel)

- Goal: Show shared rules for water, rest, and safety at a desert oasis.
- Age: 9–12 base; 15+ toggles add climate and trade context.
- Hook: Caravan bells and date palms; all circles gather to plan water turns.
- Beats: check cistern; assign turns; hear news; prepare night watch.
- Puzzles: bucket‑chain logic; ledger fairness — balance cups across groups.
- Choices/Flags: `ss1.splitPolicy = equal|need|alternate`; `ss1.nightWatch = rota|volunteer`.
- Guardrails: no graphic hardship; respectful languages; context captions.
- Assets: `bg_ss_1.avif`, `prop_ss_1_oasis_ledger.svg`, `ui_ss_meter.svg`.
- Copy notes: emphasize fairness emerges from group rules.
- Fact‑check: oasis cistern practices; caravan routines.
- Review: assets listed; captions drafted; contrast okay.

SS2 Copper to Iron

- Goal: Collective learning in metallurgy via itinerant smiths and apprentices.
- Hook: Furnace base laid; many hands prepare bellows and ore.
- Beats: gather ore/charcoal; build furnace; temper tools; share know‑how.
- Puzzles: furnace assembly; temperature balance minigame.
- Flags: `ss2.method = bloomery|shaft`; `ss2.tempering = water|oil`.
- Guardrails: abstracted furnace visuals; safety captions.
- Assets: `prop_ss_2_furnace_parts.svg`.

SS3 Wayfinding Across Dunes

- Goal: Teams plan routes using stars, winds, and dune shapes.
- Puzzles: star chart alignment; dawn/dusk march planner with heat constraints.
- Flags: `ss3.route = north|ridge|wadi`.
